# Atriopelago Pricing

Full-service marketing for small boutique tourism properties (boutique hotels 4-12 rooms, yacht charters, seasonal villas). Prices are net (excl. VAT). The Meta Ads media budget is paid separately by the client.

## Package A — Is it even worth it (test)
- Price: 1,200-1,500 PLN net
- Timeline: ~1 week
- Includes: mini landing page PL+EN for one test campaign, 5 social graphics (IG/FB), 1 short video 15-30s (AI-generated or from your footage), 7-day Meta Ads test (setup + optimisation; ad budget paid separately), report on what worked
- Best for: owners who want to test demand before committing to a full season campaign
- Note: if you upgrade to Package B within 30 days, 100% of Package A counts toward B

## Package B — Season in hand (launch)
- Price: 4,500 PLN early bird (until 31.08.2026 or first 5 signed) / 4,500-6,000 PLN by property scale
- Timeline: 2-3 weeks production, 30-day campaign
- Includes: PL+EN website (5-7 sections, written for international guests), 12-15 selected and retouched photos, 15 seasonal graphics, 3 short videos, Meta Ads (pixel setup + seasonal campaign + 30 days optimisation), IG/FB publishing calendar, GA4 + Meta Pixel analytics + monthly report, 2 rounds of copy and 2 rounds of design revisions, hosting on [property].atriopelago.pl for 12 months, handoff guide + 30-min consult
- Best for: boutique hotels, villas and charters that need a complete, campaign-ready front for one season

## Package C — Yearly marketing partner (retainer)
- Price: 2,800 / 3,300 / 3,800 PLN per month by property scale
- Minimum term: 6 months
- Includes: website upkeep, ongoing Meta Ads management, 8 social posts + 2 reels per month, 1 SEO blog post per month PL+EN, email automation, monthly report + 1h strategy call; +5% commission on confirmed direct bookings from tracked sources
- Best for: properties that want continuous marketing across the whole year, not just one campaign
